Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: May 6, 2026

This page explains how users of [Balance] can request deletion of their account and associated data.

English

If you want to delete your account and the data associated with it, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send a deletion request by email to ahmedazooz15@gmail.com.
  2. Include the email address or username linked to your account.
  3. We will review your request and complete the deletion within 7 days.

What will be deleted

  • Profile information.
  • Login credentials associated with your account.
  • Any data directly linked to your account.

What may be retained

We may keep certain information for a limited period if required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes. Any retained data will be handled according to our Privacy Policy.

Contact for deletion requests:
Email: ahmedazooz15@gmail.com
